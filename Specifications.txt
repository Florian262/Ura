3. Section-by-Section Code & Design RulesA. The Typography SystemThe Main Headline: Set your main text block to font-sans font-bold tracking-tight uppercase. Use a solid text scale ratio. The Turkish and Albanian relationship should feel architectural.The Translation Styling: Anytime a Turkish word is presented on this page as an example, the Albanian subtitle must be exactly 2 structural font-sizes smaller, styled in italic text-neutral-500 font-medium.B. Button Architecture (Anti-Slop Rule)No Rounded Corners: Avoid pill-shaped buttons or heavy rounded-xl corners. Use rounded-none or a highly conservative rounded-sm (2px). Sharp edges scream "custom professional software tool," whereas heavy roundness feels like a generic template.Hover & Active Feedback: Because this is an offline app, interaction speed is your superpower. Give your primary CTA button an immediate, crisp color shift on click:Default: bg-[#3A5A40] text-white font-medium tracking-wideActive/Tap: bg-[#2A3F2E] (immediate shift with 0ms transition lag)C. The Background "Watermark" Asset (Optional)If you want a subtle background visual element so the page doesn't feel entirely blank:Do not use an AI-generated image of a book, a mosque, or an Albanian eagle.Instead, use a pure geometric vector pattern or low-opacity typography watermark running vertically down the right edge of the screen—for example, the letters "A1 ➔ C2" typed out in a massive, ultra-light gray (text-neutral-100/40 text-9xl font-black) that sits entirely behind your text content layers.
